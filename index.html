Why It Matters

Interviews Are Learnable Skills, Not Innate Talents

Many students enter job interviews believing that success depends on personality or luck. The reality is different: interview performance is a set of learnable, practiceable skills that improve with structured preparation.

This hub was built to demystify the interview process, giving every student the frameworks, vocabulary, and confidence to walk into any interview — and perform at their best.
