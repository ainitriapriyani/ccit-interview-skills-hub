Our Process

How We Built This

A collaborative academic project built with equal parts research, design thinking, and peer feedback.

Phase 01

Research & Planning

We began by mapping the most common pain points students face in job interviews, then structured a curriculum to address each one systematically. Sources included academic literature, professional development resources, and industry surveys.

Phase 02

Content Development

Each team member was assigned specific modules based on their strengths. Weekly review sessions ensured consistency across all content, while peer editing kept the tone accessible without sacrificing academic depth.

Phase 03

Design & Build

The interface was designed to feel like a professional learning platform — clean, trustworthy, and easy to navigate. We implemented dark mode, smooth animations, and mobile responsiveness to ensure accessibility for all users.

Phase 04

Review & Refinement

Following a complete draft, we tested the site across devices, reviewed all content for accuracy, and incorporated feedback from our course supervisor. Final touches were made to polish both design and content quality.
